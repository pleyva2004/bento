# AI Automation in the Enterprise: Beyond the Hype

Every company wants to leverage AI automation. But most struggle to move beyond pilot projects. After working with dozens of enterprises, we've learned what actually works.

## Start with Clear Use Cases

Don't automate for automation's sake. The best implementations start with specific pain points:

- **High-volume repetitive tasks** - Perfect for automation
- **Time-sensitive decisions** - Where speed matters more than perfection
- **Tasks requiring consistency** - Where human variation is a problem

## Build Trust Gradually

Enterprises don't adopt new technology overnight. Our most successful implementations follow a pattern:

1. **Shadow mode** - AI makes recommendations, humans make all decisions
2. **Assisted mode** - AI makes decisions, humans review and can override
3. **Autonomous mode** - AI makes decisions independently, humans audit periodically

## Measure What Matters

Focus on business outcomes, not AI metrics. Track:

- Time saved
- Error rates
- Employee satisfaction
- Customer impact
- Financial ROI
